1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how our Tarot application ("App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any registration information. No account creation is required.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name, surname, or any personal identifiers
  • Email address or phone number
  • Location data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Contacts or social media data

3. Information Processed by Third-Party Services

While we do not collect personal information directly, our App uses third-party services that may collect certain technical data:

Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display banner and rewarded advertisements. AdMob may collect and process:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS)
  • IP address (used to determine approximate region)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is used to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings under Privacy → Tracking.

Firebase Cloud Functions (AI Interpretation)

When you request a tarot card interpretation, the selected card information is sent to our Firebase Cloud Function, which processes your request using an AI model and returns the interpretation. This data is processed in transit and not permanently stored on our servers.

Firebase Privacy Policy →

4. Data Stored on Your Device

Your past tarot readings (history) are stored locally on your device only. This data does not leave your device and is not accessible to us. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

In accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework, our App may request permission to track you across apps and websites for advertising purposes (via AdMob). You can choose to allow or deny this request. Denying tracking will result in non-personalized ads being shown instead.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

3. Üçüncü Taraf Hizmetleri Tarafından İşlenen Bilgiler

Kişisel bilgileri doğrudan toplamamakla birlikte, Uygulamamız belirli teknik verileri toplayabilecek üçüncü taraf hizmetleri kullanmaktadır:

Google AdMob (Reklamcılık)

Banner ve ödüllü reklamlar göstermek için Google AdMob kullanıyoruz. AdMob şu verileri toplayabilir ve işleyebilir:

  • Reklam Kimliği (iOS'ta IDFA)
  • IP adresi (yaklaşık bölgeyi belirlemek için kullanılır)
  • Cihaz türü ve işletim sistemi sürümü

Bu veriler ilgili reklamları sunmak ve reklam performansını ölçmek için kullanılır. Cihaz ayarlarınızdan Gizlilik → İzleme bölümüne giderek kişiselleştirilmiş reklamları devre dışı bırakabilirsiniz.

Firebase Cloud Functions (Yapay Zeka Yorumu)

Bu isteği kabul edebilir veya reddedebilirsiniz. İzlemeyi reddetmeniz durumunda kişiselleştirilmemiş reklamlar gösterilecektir.
